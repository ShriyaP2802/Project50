Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Camels

  • Camels are known as the ships of the Desert
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat

Cicadas

  1. Cicadas live on all continents except Antarctica
  2. Cicadas can’t eat as they lack jaws. Instead they get nutrients from tree roots
  3. Some cicadas could explode with fungus

Pandas

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim
  • Pandas go from pink to white and black as they age
  • An adult panda can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day
